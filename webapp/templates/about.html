About Femicide Watch

Femicide Watch is a coordinated response platform that blends human-centered intake with multilingual AI triage. It was built for organizations that rely on trusted field officers to collect sensitive testimonies from survivors and witnesses.

By pairing a curated dataset with XLM-RoBERTa, the system predicts whether a narrative describes physical, sexual, emotional, or economic violence. These insights accelerate case prioritization and reveal hotspots that demand policy attention.

Our commitments

  • Respect survivor privacy and agency.
  • Empower vetted responders, not anonymous submissions.
  • Provide actionable dashboards for decision-makers.

Technology stack

  • Flask backend with secure authentication.
  • XLM-RoBERTa fine-tuned on bilingual GBV narratives.
  • SQLite/PostgreSQL storage with auditable records.
